Why video · why now

  • Events are perishable. A promo that ships late fills zero seats — and every center runs dozens of trainings a year.
  • Design capacity is scarce. Most centers have no designer; DIY templates drift off-brand and eat advisor hours.
  • Video wins the feed. The platforms our clients actually use reward motion — exactly what's hardest to make.
The solution

  • Promo Studio. A single HTML file — open in Chrome, type your event, export MP4 + graphics. Nothing to install, nothing uploaded.
  • A real design system. Layouts that make every center's post look designed.
  • Claude tailors it. Same card, two brands — one small file of tokens apart.
