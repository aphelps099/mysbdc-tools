A free video + graphics tool for SBDC networks — and a live walkthrough of how Claude customizes it to your brand, while you watch.

The problem — and why now

Great trainings, invisible promotion

  • Events are perishable. A promo that ships late fills zero seats — and every center runs dozens of trainings a year.
  • Design capacity is scarce. Most centers have no designer; DIY templates drift off-brand and eat advisor hours.
  • Video wins the feed. The platforms our clients actually use reward motion — and that's exactly what's hardest to make.
The solution

One small tool, one design system, and Claude

  • Promo Studio. A single HTML file — open it in Chrome, type your event, export an MP4 and matching graphics. No accounts, no installs, nothing uploaded.
  • A real design system. Save-the-date, agenda, and end-card layouts that make every center's post look designed — with your fonts, colors, and logo.
  • Claude does the tailoring. You describe your brand; Claude explores identities and hands the winning one straight into the tool.
