Newsletter Transformation

The current newsletter underperforms with a 0.7% click-through rate—well below industry benchmarks. The newsletter is our single highest-leverage owned channel and costs nothing to improve.

Current State
  • Generic, one-size-fits-all content
  • Event listings dominate the content
  • No personalization or advisor connection
  • Inconsistent send schedule
  • No clear brand voice
  • No TFG or program-specific content
New Approach
  • Segmented by region, industry, and client stage
  • Story-led content with events as secondary CTA
  • Advisor spotlight + "reply to your advisor" prompt
  • Bi-weekly cadence with consistent format
  • YBP tone: warm, knowledgeable, action-oriented
  • Rotating spotlights: TFG, Manufacturing, Capital Advisors

Section 1

Lead Story

Client success story or advisor profile — People/Funded/Connected pillar rotation

Section 2

Quick Tip

One actionable business insight — tax deadline, SBA checklist, marketing tactic

Section 3

Program Spotlight

Rotating feature — TFG, Manufacturing, Capital Advisors, or workshops

Also In Each Issue

  • Upcoming Events — Top 3–5 workshops/webinars with clear registration CTAs
  • Community Connection — Partner highlight, local resource, or network news

Newsletter Targets

  • Click-through rate from 0.7% to 3%+ within 6 months
  • Grow subscriber list by 20%
  • Achieve 30%+ open rate consistently
  • Become a measurable driver of registrations and inquiries
