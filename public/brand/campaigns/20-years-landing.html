Twenty years of showing up.

Recessions, pandemics, fires, floods. We've been here through all of it — helping businesses survive, adapt, and grow.

2006
The Beginning
Northern California SBDC network launches with a handful of centers and a simple mission: help small businesses succeed.
2008
The Recession
When banks stopped lending, we helped businesses find capital, restructure, and survive the worst financial crisis in decades.
2020
The Pandemic
PPP applications, pivots to e-commerce, survival planning. Our advisors worked overtime to keep businesses alive.
2026
Still Here
18 centers. 300+ advisors. $547M in capital accessed last year. Free. Always.
