Primary Tagline

Variant · Connected
Your Business, Connected.
Usage Notes
  • Use the primary tagline (Your Business, Better.) as the default in all brand collateral, presentations, and external messaging.
  • Deploy People when emphasizing advising relationships, mentorship, or the human side of the network.
  • Use Funded in lender-facing materials, capital access campaigns, and financial readiness messaging.
  • Use Connected when highlighting partnerships, referral networks, or ecosystem collaboration.
  • Always include the period. Always include the comma before the final word. The structure is: Your Business, [Word].
Voice & Tone
Four attributes that define how NorCal SBDC sounds in every communication.
Expert
not academic
We know small business inside and out. We share knowledge practically, using language entrepreneurs actually use. No jargon, no theory for theory's sake.
Warm
not casual
We care about the people behind the businesses. Our tone is approachable and encouraging, but always professional. We are advisors, not buddies.
Confident
not boastful
We let our impact numbers speak. $547M in capital accessed. 7,500+ entrepreneurs served. We state facts clearly without overselling or hedging.
Action-oriented
not passive
Every piece of messaging should move someone toward a next step. We connect, prepare, advise, and grow. Our language does something.
Elevator Pitches
Three audience-specific pitches. Copy the one you need.
For Entrepreneurs
NorCal SBDC provides no-fee, confidential business advising and training across 36 counties in Northern California. Whether you're starting up or scaling, we connect you with expert advisors, capital resources, and the tools to grow.
For Stakeholders & Legislators
The NorCal SBDC network has helped small businesses access over $547M in capital and serves 7,500+ clients annually across 36 Northern California counties. Our no-fee advising model generates measurable ROI through job creation, revenue growth, and business starts.
For Lenders & Partners
We prepare loan-ready clients with complete packages, realistic expectations, and solid financials. Our 18 centers across Northern California send you borrowers who are ready to close.
