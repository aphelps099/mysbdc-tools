Oliver & Grace Estrada · Marin SBDC

When the pandemic forced Oliver and Grace Estrada to close their brick-and-mortar stores, they followed their instinct to do what they love. Starting Yarrow Goods from a spare bedroom in 2020, they built a handmade screen-printed t-shirt brand with an emphasis on ethical manufacturing and sustainability. With guidance from Marin SBDC Advisor Richard Strezo, they secured capital from five different lenders and grew sales by 20%.

Newsletter Template Guidelines

Photo First

Always lead with a client photo — it drives 2× more engagement than text-only emails.

Under 150 Words

The narrative should be 3-4 sentences max. Link to the full story for the rest.

3 Metrics, Always

Pick the 3 most compelling numbers. Capital accessed should always be one of them.

Quote Block

One direct quote from the business owner. Specific > generic.

CTA → Full Story

Every newsletter feature should drive to the full story page on norcalsbdc.org

Send Frequency

Growth stories work best in monthly partner updates. Signature stories can be standalone sends.
