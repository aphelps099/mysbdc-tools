A Message From Our Director

Dear Partners,

For 20 years, NorCal SBDC has helped small businesses access the capital they need to start, grow, and thrive.

None of this happens without partners like you.

Our lending partners don't just provide capital—they provide confidence. When a business owner walks into your office with a solid plan, accurate financials, and clear goals, that's our advisors at work. When they walk out with funding, that's partnership at work.

This guide outlines how we can work together to support the businesses that make our region stronger.
