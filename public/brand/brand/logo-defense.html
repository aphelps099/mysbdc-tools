20Years

Milestone Moment

Twenty years of service. $547M in capital accessed last year alone. This isn't a rebrand—it's an earned evolution. We've proven ourselves. Now our logo should reflect that confidence.

Digital Reality

In 2006, logos lived on letterheads. In 2026, they live in 32×32 pixel favicons, social avatars, and email signatures. Complexity doesn't scale down. Simplicity does.

Brand Recognition

The swoosh with the star is our brand asset. Not "America's." Not "California." The swoosh. Let it breathe. Let it be remembered.

Network Alignment

Other California SBDC networks are already moving this direction. We're not being radical—we're catching up to modern brand standards.
