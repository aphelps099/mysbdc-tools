Centralized Intake Process

Client Journey

01
Online Application
Client submits an online application, generating a signed SBA 641 form automatically captured in Neoserra.
02
Phone Intake
Client books a 20-minute phone intake via Calendly. CI Specialist gathers baseline data, assesses readiness, and explains EI collection.
03
Match or Refer
Ready clients are matched with an advisor by goals, industry, language, and location. Others are referred to workshops or partners and can return later.
04
Ongoing Advising
No-fee sessions as often as needed. Capital access support, specialist referrals, workshop recommendations, and accountability toward milestones.
05
Impact & Growth
Milestones are tracked via Neoserra: capital secured, jobs created, revenue grown. EI data captured through the Milestone Attribution API.

How We Operate

Platforms & Tools

Client Management
Neoserra
System of record. Intake forms, session tracking, milestone logging, satisfaction surveys, EI attribution, and real-time performance dashboards. Reviewed monthly, quarterly, and annually.
Impact Tracking

Captures and syncs economic impact data into Neoserra. Advisor communication during sessions, email survey via landing page, and annual API survey for 3+ hour clients. Spot-checked throughout the year.

Client-facing scheduling for the centralized intake process. Clients self-book their 20-minute phone intake, reducing friction from first contact to first conversation.
