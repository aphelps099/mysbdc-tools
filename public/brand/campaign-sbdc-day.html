Event Starter Kit

Open House
Invite the community into your center. Offer tours, meet-the-advisor sessions, and light refreshments. Keep it informal.

Campaign Lockup

NorCal SBDC logo stacked above "SBDC Day 2026." Horizontal variant for wide formats. Minimum 20px clear space. Never alter proportions.

Photography

Real over stock. Authentic photos of advisors and clients in consultations. Candid, natural, diverse. No generic business imagery.

Co-Branding

Include "In coordination with America's SBDC" on major graphics. NorCal SBDC primary, America's SBDC supporting. Tag @americassbdc on social.

Tone & Language

Headlines in Tobias: warm, proud. Body in GT America Light: conversational, direct. Always "no-fee" — never "free." Close with "Your Business, Better."
