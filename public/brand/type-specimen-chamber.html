Our Mission

Twenty years of building what matters

The Northern California SBDC exists for one reason: to give every entrepreneur in our region the advising, capital connections, and tools they need to grow.

We work across 36 counties, from the Bay Area to the Oregon border. Our advisors bring real-world business experience, not theory. They've built companies, secured funding, navigated regulations, and scaled operations.

Every engagement starts with listening. We meet entrepreneurs where they are — whether that's a kitchen-table idea or a $5M revenue plateau — and help them find the next step forward.

Our Approach

Meet them where they are

We don’t hand entrepreneurs a generic playbook. Every advising engagement begins with a deep assessment of the business: its financials, its market position, its team, and the specific obstacles standing between now and the next milestone.

From there, we build a roadmap together. Some clients need one session. Others work with us for years. The relationship adapts because growth isn’t linear.
