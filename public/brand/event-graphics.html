Logo Placement Rules
Do: Top-left with clear space
Position logo in top-left or centered with at least 1x logo-height of clear space on all sides.
Do: Correct color contrast
Use white logo on dark backgrounds, blue logo on light backgrounds. Never reverse this pairing.
Don't: Stretch, rotate, or recolor
Never distort the logo proportions, rotate it, or apply colors outside the approved palette.
Don't: Place on busy backgrounds
Avoid placing the logo on patterned, photographic, or low-contrast backgrounds without a solid backing.

Branding Checklist
  • Logo present and correctly placed
  • norcalsbdc.org URL included on every piece
  • "No-fee" language used (never "free")
  • Tagline present: Your Business, Better.
  • Correct fonts: Tobias (headlines) + GT America (body)
  • Color palette matches brand tokens (Navy, Pool, Royal, Brick, Cream)
  • Contact info included: phelps@norcalsbdc.org
  • Print files exported at 300 DPI minimum
