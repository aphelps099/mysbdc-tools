Talking Points
Use these when reaching out to lenders, CDFIs, and economic development partners.
For Banks & Lenders
We don't compete with you — we make your pipeline stronger. SBDC referrals come with clean financials, solid business plans, and realistic loan amounts. Our advisors do the prep work so your underwriters can close faster.
For CDFIs
Same mission, different tools. You lend, we advise. Together we reach the businesses that need capital most. Our 18 centers across NorCal are embedded in the communities you serve.
For Cities & Counties
Your constituents, our advisors. No-fee small business support for your community. We serve 36 counties and generate measurable economic impact: job creation, revenue growth, and business starts.
The ROI Conversation
$58 back for every $1 invested — that's the SBA-verified return. Our no-fee advising model doesn't just help entrepreneurs, it creates measurable economic impact that benefits the entire lending ecosystem.
