Today, we celebrate something bigger than a date on the calendar. We celebrate twenty years of showing up—in community centers, coffee shops, and conference rooms across Northern California—to help people turn ideas into businesses.

Sacramento County
From food truck to three locations.
Maria came to us with a food truck, a family recipe, and a question: "How do I grow?" Her advisor helped her build a business plan, secure an SBA loan, and navigate her first commercial lease. Five years later, she has three locations and 22 employees.

When COVID shut down his primary customer, Tom thought he'd have to close his 15-year manufacturing business. His SBDC advisor helped him pivot—identifying new markets, reworking his supply chain, and connecting him with government contracts. Revenue is now 40% higher than pre-pandemic.
