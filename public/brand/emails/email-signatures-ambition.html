Quick Setup Guide

For Gmail / Outlook (Web)

  1. Go to Settings → Signatures
  2. Copy the signature HTML directly from this preview
  3. Paste into the signature editor
  4. Replace placeholder info with your details

For Bold/Dark Signatures (S4, S6)

  1. These work best as images for maximum compatibility
  2. Export as PNG at 2x resolution
  3. Insert as image in your email signature settings
  4. Link the image to norcalsbdc.org

Recommendation

S1 (Clean Line) for everyday use — it works across all email clients without issues.
S5 (ZAG Editorial) for relationship-building — warm and human.
S6 (Digital ZAG) for tech programs — bold statement piece.
