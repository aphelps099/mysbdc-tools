Week-Long Activities

Event Starter Kit

NSBW is a full week — centers should pick 2–4 activities that work for their team and audience. Mix in-person with virtual for maximum reach.

Capital Access Workshop
90-min hybrid session on SBA loans, grants, and alternative funding. 60 min content + 30 min Q&A. Great for startup and growth-stage founders.
Hybrid90 minMon/Tue
Scaling Your Team Webinar
Virtual-only session on hiring, systems, and sustainable growth. 45 min content + 15 min Q&A. Target scale-up and mid-market founders.
Virtual60 minWed
Networking Mixer
Evening event — founders, advisors, and partners. Light appetizers, informal networking. 2 hours, low-lift, high-energy.
In-PersonEveningWed
Awards Ceremony
Honor exceptional entrepreneurs with hybrid ceremony. Nominee spotlights, keynote, recognition. Livestream for virtual attendees.
HybridEveningFri
Client Story Videos
Pre-record 60-second clips with 3–5 clients sharing their SBDC experience. Release one per day across social channels.
ContentPre-RecordDaily
Social Media Takeover
Different center takes over the NorCal SBDC account each day. Behind-the-scenes, advisor intros, local flavor.

Campaign Lockup

NorCal SBDC logo stacked above "National Small Business Week 2026." Horizontal variant for wide formats. Abbreviated "NSBW 2026" for small spaces. 20px minimum clear space.

Photography

Real over stock. Entrepreneurs in their workspaces, candid consultation moments, workshop action shots. Diverse founders reflecting NorCal's communities.

SBA Co-Branding

Include "In partnership with the U.S. Small Business Administration" on major graphics. SBA seal in hero (lower right, 120px). NorCal SBDC primary, SBA supporting.

Tone & Language

Headlines in Tobias: bold, inspirational. Body in GT America Light: clear, conversational. Always "no-fee" — never "free." Close with "Your Business, Better."
