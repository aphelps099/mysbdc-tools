Suggested Roles per Typeface

Display headings

GT Era Display

Welcome title, About overlay heading, empty-state hero. Replaces Tobias for a cleaner, more contemporary feel.

UI body copy

GT Era Text

Chat bubbles, descriptions, sub-headings. Medium weight reads well at 14-16 px. Can complement or replace GT America for body.

Accent / kicker

Pressura Ext.

Short uppercase labels, section dividers, or dramatic emphasis. Use sparingly — one or two words at a time.
