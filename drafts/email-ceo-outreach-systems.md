Wanted to share some updates on what we've been up to on the tech side — and pick your brain on a couple things.

We've been building a tools layer on top of Neoserra and it's come together really well. The big pieces:

- **Form wizards** for intake — multi-step, Typeform-style flows for TFG, 641, and R4I that create Neoserra records automatically. Advisors don't have to touch the CRM for intake anymore.
- **Milestone collection + an impact dashboard** — clients self-report milestones through a guided flow, and it feeds a real-time dashboard our leadership uses for reporting.

The team loves it and it's made a real difference in how we operate.

The thing is, as we've built this out, we've bumped into some gaps in the API. Two areas that would be huge for us (and honestly for any SBDC host):

1. **Aggregate milestone data** — being able to pull 12-month milestones by type (capital, jobs, revenue, new businesses) and by impact. Right now we're piecing it together manually. A clean endpoint for this would unlock real reporting for everyone.

2. **Training endpoints** — registration and attendance. There's no good programmatic way to work with training data right now, and it's a big part of what we report to SBA.
