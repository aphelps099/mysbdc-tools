ORGANIZATION OVERVIEW
Tech Futures Group (TFG) is a specialized venture advisory program designed to support early-stage technology startups. TFG is "your strategic partner in innovation," transforming sector-specific challenges into growth opportunities.

MISSION & PURPOSE
TFG operates as a no-cost, no-equity advisory service for select technology companies located within the Northern California Small Business Development Center (SBDC) network coverage area. Their goal is to "drive innovation and growth throughout California."

LEADERSHIP
Matthew JoEhnk serves as Director of Tech Futures Group.

CORE SERVICE AREAS

Three Specialized Expertise Domains:

1. Product-Market Fit
   - Go-to-market strategy
   - Customer discovery and validation
   - Positioning
   - Commercialization

2. Finance & Intellectual Property
   - Financial strategy
   - Financial modeling
   - Intellectual property guidance

3. Grants Support
   - SBIR/STTR grant navigation
   - Application support

TARGET STARTUP STAGE
TFG identifies its "sweet spot" as companies progressing from idea stage through product-market fit, specifically targeting:
- Founding teams
- Live MVP in-market validation
- Established products with paying customers
- Growth-stage companies

SERVICE MODEL
Selected applicants work one-on-one with advisors matched by industry, maturity level, and specific needs. The program emphasizes flexibility, allowing founders to set meeting frequency and cadence. As businesses evolve, founders typically engage multiple advisors addressing shifting priorities.

ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE & FUNDING
TFG operates under the Northern California SBDC, receiving partial funding through the Governor's Office of Business and Economic Development and cooperative agreements with the U.S. Small Business Administration.
