NorCal SBDC — ProBiz Government Contracting Program (norcalsbdc.org/probiz)

PROGRAM OVERVIEW
ProBiz is a collaborative initiative between NorCal SBDC, California Chamber of Commerce, California Hispanic Chambers of Commerce, NorCal APEX Accelerators, California Capital, and Cal Asian Chambers of Commerce.

MISSION: Grow your small business by tapping into the lucrative world of state, municipal, and corporate contracts.

The program helps entrepreneurs access government and corporate contracting opportunities through comprehensive training, exclusive networking, and financial support.

CORE SERVICES (All No-Cost)

1. Certification Assistance
   - Guidance through the certification process to bid on contracts
   - Demystification of complex requirements
   - Preparation for state agency, local municipality, and corporate certifications

2. Training & Workshops
   - Quarterly training on securing contracts
   - Virtual and in-person sessions
   - Bid preparation and review assistance
   - Industry-specific regulatory guidance

3. One-on-One Advising
   - Personalized consulting from experienced advisors
   - Tailored strategies specific to each business
   - Problem-solving for contracting challenges

4. Funding Connection
   - Connection to grants, loans, and investment opportunities
   - Support for fulfilling large contracts financially
   - Access to SBDC Finance Center

5. Networking & Partnership
   - Connection with buyers and contracting opportunities
   - Access to partner networks
   - Procurement fair participation

KEY BENEFITS
- No-fee model: All services provided at no cost
- Expert guidance: Regulatory and industry knowledge
- Comprehensive support: From certification through contract fulfillment
- Community access: Connections with other entrepreneurs and buyers

UPCOMING EVENTS
- California Department of General Services (DGS) Small Business Certification workshops
- Procurement fairs
- Bilingual training sessions
