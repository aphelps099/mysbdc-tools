NorCal SBDC — SBDC Health Program (norcalsbdc.org/sbdc-health)

PROGRAM OVERVIEW
SBDC Health is a 10-week online learning program designed to help clinician entrepreneurs transition from clinical practice to business ownership. The initiative transforms healthcare professionals "From Clinician to CEO."

TARGET AUDIENCE
Healthcare professionals looking to start or grow private practices, clinics, health-tech businesses, or other healthcare ventures.

KEY TOPICS COVERED
- Business plan development
- Private practice paperwork and regulatory requirements
- Financial sustainability and fundraising
- Workplace culture building
- Digital marketing strategies
- Human resources management
- Client acquisition
- Medical billing
- AB5 contractor law
- Capital access for healthcare businesses
- AI overview for healthcare
- AWS training

PROGRAM STRUCTURE
- Format: 10 interactive webinar sessions plus office hours
- Duration: 10 weeks with live Wednesday workshops
- Office Hours: Thursdays, 12-1 PM for customized support
- Cost: $199
- Completion: Live participation required to earn certificate

2026 COHORT SCHEDULE
Sessions run January 28 — April 2, 2026

IMPACT (from brand documents)
- 157 jobs created
- $6.9M sales increase
- $2M capital infusion
- 91 cohort graduates

PARTNERSHIP
Program developed in partnership with AWS (Amazon Web Services).

PODCAST RESOURCE
"If Healthcare Were a Business" podcast series features healthcare entrepreneurs sharing leadership insights.
