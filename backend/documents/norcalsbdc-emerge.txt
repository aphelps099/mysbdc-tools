NorCal SBDC — EMERGE Conference (norcalsbdc.org/emerge)

PROGRAM OVERVIEW
EMERGE is NorCal SBDC's professional development event designed for business advisors and entrepreneurs. The program brings together 200+ business leaders and advisors with 30+ expert speakers delivering 16 expert-led breakout sessions.

EVENT DETAILS
Date & Location: October 8-9, 2025 | Walnut Creek Marriott

TWO PRIMARY TRACKS

Track 1 — Capital & Funding
Participants explore traditional and alternative lending strategies, including:
- SBA loan programs
- Investor readiness
- Business plan development
- Agricultural lending opportunities

Track 2 — AI Innovation
Sessions address:
- Artificial intelligence fundamentals
- Practical business applications
- Ethical considerations
- Implementation strategies for operational efficiency

SCHEDULE
- October 8: Evening mixer (5:30-7:30 PM) for casual networking
- October 9: Full day including breakfast, morning/afternoon breakout sessions, opening ceremony, keynote lunch, and closing reception

SESSION TOPICS INCLUDE
- "Cracking the Code: SBA Loans Made Simple"
- Investor readiness workshops
- AI tools for business advising
