How It Works

System Overview

The Bulk Campaign Attribution Collection system digitizes milestone reporting by combining email marketing, a smart online form, and API-driven CRM integration. It replaces manual data entry with a client-facing experience that automates validation, record creation, and administrative review.

Phase 01
Send
Build a client list, upload to Constant Contact, and send a bulk email campaign with a personalized URL that pre-populates the sign-in form with the recipient's email address.
Phase 02
Collect
Clients click through to the SBDC Success portal, verify their identity, select their business, and report economic impact data through dynamically rendered fields based on business status.
Phase 03
Sync
On submission, milestones are automatically entered into Neoserra and simultaneous notifications are sent to the milestone manager, center director, and client — all without manual entry.
Step 1 · Outreach

Bulk Campaign Setup

Step A
Build the Client List
Pull client records from Neoserra for clients with 3+ counseling hours who are eligible for impact reporting. Filter by active status, service date range, and center assignment.
Source: Neoserra client export → CSV → Constant Contact upload
Step B
Upload to Constant Contact
Import the filtered client list into a dedicated Constant Contact contact list. This list becomes the audience for the attribution email campaign.
Platform: Constant Contact → Contact Management → Import
Step C
Compose the Campaign Email
Create an email with a clear call-to-action: "Share Your Business Growth!" The CTA button uses a special URL parameter that captures the recipient's Constant Contact email address.
URL parameter auto-populates the sign-in form email field on click
Step D
Send & Monitor
Schedule or send the campaign immediately. Monitor open rates, click-throughs, and form completions. Follow up with non-responders in a second wave if needed.
Track: Opens → Clicks → Form Starts → Submissions
Step 2 · Client Experience

Client Journey

1
Email Click
Client receives the campaign email and clicks the CTA. The special URL pre-populates their email into the sign-in form on the SBDC Success page.
2
Contact Details
Form collects first name, last name, email (pre-filled), and phone number. Client confirms their identity.
3
API Validation
The API locates and validates the client's business records in milliseconds using the provided email. Client selects their business from the returned list.
4
Report Impact
Dynamic fields appear based on business status (In-Business vs. Pre-Venture). Client reports capital, jobs, sales, and shares how the SBDC helped.
Step 5

Client signs the digital signature field to certify the accuracy of their reported milestones.
Step 6

A modal window displays a summary of all inputs. Client reviews the data and clicks "Confirm / Continue" to finalize the submission.
Step 7
Submission
The form submits and the system simultaneously creates the milestone record in Neoserra, triggers admin notifications, and sends the client a thank-you email.
Step 8
Confirmation
Done. Confetti. The client sees a visual confirmation that their milestones have been received and are under review.

Under the Hood

The Attribution API

Automated Record Validation & Milestone Creation
The API is the engine behind the form. It validates client identity, retrieves business records, dynamically adjusts fields based on client status, creates milestone records in Neoserra, and triggers all downstream notifications — in real time.
GET Client email → API locates matching business records in Neoserra
VALIDATE Cross-references contact details against existing client data
ROUTE Determines In-Business vs. Pre-Venture → renders correct fields
POST Creates milestone record in Neoserra on form submission
NOTIFY Sends alerts to milestone manager, center director, and client
Millisecond Lookup
Client records are located and validated instantly via the API's GET parameters using the email address.
Dynamic Routing
In-Business clients skip the "business start" question. Pre-Venture clients see it first, then capital/jobs/sales.
URL Pre-Population
The Constant Contact CTA URL embeds the recipient's email, which flows through to the sign-in form automatically.
SBA Compliance
Pre-Venture "in business" criteria follow SBA standards before capital and employment fields unlock.

Step 5 · CRM Workflow

Neoserra Integration

Milestones submitted through the form are automatically entered into Neoserra, where they await review and approval. This eliminates manual data entry and ensures a clean audit trail from client submission to final approval.

Auto
Record Creation
The API creates a new milestone record in Neoserra the instant the form is submitted. No CSV import, no manual entry — the record simply appears.
Review
Manager Assessment
The milestone manager accesses the record via the direct link in their notification email. They review data accuracy, make adjustments if needed, and move toward approval.
Approve
Final Approval
Once reviewed, the milestone is approved in Neoserra. Records over $1M are spot-checked before the annual NEXUS upload to ensure accuracy and compliance.
