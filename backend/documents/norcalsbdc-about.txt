NorCal SBDC — About Page (norcalsbdc.org/about)

MISSION & PURPOSE
The Norcal SBDC empowers entrepreneurs on their journey — providing vital guidance on growth strategies, business planning, accessing capital, marketing, management, scaling, and more.

SERVICE AREA

IMPACT METRICS (FFY 2022-24)
- $1.56 billion in capital investment for clients
- 7,000+ new jobs created
- 1,900+ ventures launched
- $474 million in additional revenue generated
- 111,000 local jobs supported
- 99% client satisfaction rate

FUNDING & GOVERNANCE
Services provided at no cost to clients, funded through partnerships including the U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) and California Governor's Office of Business and Economic Development (Cal OSBA). Hosted by Cal Poly Humboldt Sponsored Programs Foundation.
