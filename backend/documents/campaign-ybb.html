Campaign Lockup

NorCal SBDC logo with "Your Business, Better." tagline beneath. Horizontal variant for wide formats. "People. Funded. Connected." appears as a secondary line when space allows. Never alter proportions or reorder the pillars.

Photography

Real over stock. Authentic photos of advisors and clients in consultations, workshops, and community moments. Candid, natural, diverse. Each pillar has a visual lane: People = faces and handshakes, Funded = signing moments and growth, Connected = groups and events.

Pillar Color Coding

People = Royal (#1D5AA7). Funded = Brick (#a82039). Connected = Pool (#8FC5D9). Use as accent only — pillar tags, borders, icon tints. Never as full background floods. Navy remains the primary brand color.

Tone & Language

Headlines in Tobias: warm, confident, human. Body in GT America Light: conversational, direct, no jargon. Always "no-fee" — never "free." Close with "Your Business, Better." The pillars are "People, Funded, Connected" — always in that order.
