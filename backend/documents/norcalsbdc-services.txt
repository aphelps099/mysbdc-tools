NorCal SBDC — Services (norcalsbdc.org/services)

CORE SERVICES

Confidential Advising
NorCal SBDC provides no-fee, confidential, one-to-one advising services covering business plan development, marketing strategy, sales guidance, and financial management assistance for small business owners throughout Northern California.

Training Programs
The organization delivers interactive training available online and in-person on topics including social media marketing, exporting, and government contracting. Last year they hosted 754 trainings reaching over 13,300 entrepreneurs.

Recorded Webinars
For busy entrepreneurs, the SBDC offers on-demand webinar content providing business insights accessible on flexible schedules, covering various operational topics.

SPECIALIZED PROGRAMS

Tech Futures Group (TFG)
Provides expert guidance specifically for technology-focused businesses, offering strategic advice and growth opportunities in rapidly evolving tech sectors. Verticals: Software & SaaS, BioTech, Climate Tech.

EATS (Food & Hospitality)
Dedicated restaurant and food service support covering strategy, operations management, and marketing for culinary businesses.

Finance Center
Specialized financing consultants guide loan packaging processes and connect entrepreneurs with over 100 financial institution partners to identify appropriate funding solutions. Services include SBA loan navigation, financial projections, cash flow planning, and lender matching.

Government Contracting — ProBiz
State and local government contracting program. Bid preparation, compliance, procurement navigation. Collaborative initiative between NorCal SBDC, California Chamber of Commerce, California Hispanic Chambers of Commerce, NorCal APEX Accelerators, California Capital, and Cal Asian Chambers of Commerce.

Government Contracting — APEX Accelerator
Federal contracting support (formerly PTAC). Helps small businesses identify, bid on, and win federal, state, local, and tribal agency contracts.

RESOURCE MATERIALS
- Digital Guide to Starting a Business: Covers planning, finance, legal requirements, permitting, hiring, operations, and marketing fundamentals
- Digital Guide to Growing Your Business: Addresses competitive positioning, business plan updates, branding strategies, and market expansion opportunities

REGIONAL NETWORK
16 advising centers located throughout Northern California, including specialized offices in major urban areas and rural regions.
